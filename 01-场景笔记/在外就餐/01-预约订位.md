# 预约订位

## 对话 1：普通预约（成功）

**A (顾客):** Hi, I'd like to make a reservation for Saturday evening.  
**B (餐厅):** Sure, how many people?  
**A:** Four. Around seven if you have it.  
**B:** Seven o'clock for four... we've got a table at seven, or seven-thirty.  
**A:** Seven works.  
**B:** Can I get your name and a phone number?  
**A:** Yeah, it's Mark Chen, five-five-five-oh-one-nine-two.  
**B:** Got it, Mr. Chen. Saturday at seven for four. See you then.

> **连读 / 弱读笔记**
> - **I'd like to** → /aɪd laɪk tə/，口语最常用弱化，不会读成 /tuː/
> - **make a reservation** → 连贯朗读，重音在 reservation 中段
> - **we've got** → /wiːv ɡɑːt/，老外极速连读
> - **Seven works** → 极简地道口语：七点可以（替代死板的 Seven is okay）

## 对话 2：满座，改时间

**A:** Hey, do you have a table for two tonight?  
**B:** Let me check... What time were you thinking?  
**A:** Around eight.  
**B:** We're fully booked at eight, but I can do eight-thirty or nine.  
**A:** Nine's fine.  
**B:** Nine for two, under what name?  
**A:** Sarah Lin.  
**B:** All right, Sarah. We'll hold the table until nine-fifteen.

> **语法：What time were you thinking?**
> - **完整原句**：What time were you thinking **of coming**?
>   - 口语简化：What time were you thinking?（省略 of coming）
> - **核心语法**：
>   - `were thinking`：过去进行时，语气委婉，不是真的"过去"在想
>   - `think of doing sth`：of 是介词，后面动词变 -ing → think of coming
>   - 问句结构：疑问词提前 + were 放主语前
> - **同义句**：
>   - What time did you have in mind?
>   - What time were you hoping for?

## 对话 3：特殊要求

**A:** Hi, I want to book a table for my **anniversary**（周年纪念日）dinner. Three people, Friday night.  
**B:** Congrats. Any time preference?  
**A:** Around seven-thirty. And is it possible to get a quiet table? Maybe by the window?  
**B:** Let me see what we can do. Yeah, we have a corner table available. I'll note that down. Name?  
**A:** David Wang.  
**B:** Okay, David. Friday at seven-thirty, three people, corner table. Anything else?  
**A:** No, that's it. Thanks.  
**B:** See you Friday.

> **扩展：餐厅座位 & 特殊需求 完整清单**
>
> **一、座位位置类**
> - **by the window** 靠窗座位
>   - Could we get a table by the window, please?
> - **a quiet table** 安静的位置
>   - We'd like a quiet table away from noisy crowds.
> - **corner table** 角落桌
>   - Is a corner table available for three people?
> - **away from the kitchen / kitchen door** 远离厨房
>   - Can we sit somewhere far from the kitchen door?
> - **away from the restrooms** 远离卫生间
>   - Please give us a table not near the restrooms.
> - **away from the bar** 远离吧台
>   - We prefer seats away from the bar area.
> - **not near the speakers / music** 避开音响
>   - Could we avoid tables next to the speakers?
> - **outdoor seating / a table outside** 户外座位
>   - Do you still have outdoor seating available tonight?
> - **indoor table** 室内座位
>   - We'd rather take an indoor table instead of outside.
> - **booth seat** 卡座
>   - Can we reserve a booth seat for our anniversary?
> - **high-top table** 高脚桌
>   - We want a high-top table near the window.
> - **table with more space** 宽敞桌子
>   - Could we get a table with more extra space?
> - **close to the entrance** 靠近门口
>   - Please arrange a table close to the entrance.
>
> **二、桌椅 & 人数相关**
> - **a larger table with extra chairs** 加大桌子、多加椅子
>   - We need a larger table with two extra chairs.
> - **connected tables for groups** 多人拼桌
>   - We have eight guests, can you put two tables together?
>
> **三、环境设备需求**
> - **table with a charging port** 带充电口的桌子
>   - Is there a table with a charging port nearby?
> - **dim lighting / not too bright** 光线柔和、别太亮
>   - We hope our table has dim lighting.
> - **table with heating lamp**（户外）带取暖灯户外桌
>   - For the outdoor table, could we have one with a heating lamp?
>
> **四、其他附加需求**
> - **no strong perfume candles** 不要浓香味蜡烛
>   - Please don't put strong scented candles on our table.
> - **high chair for kid** 儿童餐椅
>   - Could you prepare a high chair for our little kid?
> - **dog-friendly outdoor seat** 可带宠物户外位
>   - Do you have dog-friendly outdoor tables?

## 对话 4：临时加人

**A:** Hi, I have a reservation under Lisa Zhang for tonight at six-thirty for four. Actually, we're gonna need one more seat. Can you add a person?  
**B:** Let me check... Yeah, we can squeeze in a fifth. I'll update your reservation.  
**A:** Great, thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **I have a reservation under [name]** — 打电话报预约名的万能句型。under 表示"名字登记在…下面"
> - **squeeze in a fifth** — 硬塞下第五个人。squeeze in 是"勉强安排/塞进去"，数字直接用 a fifth 不说 a fifth person
> - **gonna need** — going to need 的口语缩读，比 will need 更自然

## 对话 5：取消预约

**A:** Hi, I need to cancel my reservation for tomorrow. It's under James Wu, six people at seven.  
**B:** James Wu... got it. I've cancelled that for you.  
**A:** Thanks. We might come back next week.  
**B:** No worries, just give us a call.

> **语言点**
> - **cancel my reservation** — 取消预约，最直接的说法。也可以说 I need to change / push back my reservation（改时间）
> - **It's under [name]** — 同上，under 表登记名
> - **No worries, just give us a call** — 轻松回应 + 邀请再联系，服务行业常用客套

## 对话 6：打电话确认营业时间

**A:** Hi, is this The Golden Fork?  
**B:** Yes, it is. How can I help you?  
**A:** I was wondering if you're still open on Sunday evenings?  
**B:** We are, until ten. Did you want to make a reservation?  
**A:** Yeah, for four people, around seven.  
**B:** Sure, let me get your details.  
**A:** It's under Tony Ma. And can we get a window table?  
**B:** I'll put you down for Sunday at seven, four people, window table. We'll see you then.  
**A:** Perfect, thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **Is this [restaurant name]?** — 打电话确认对方身份的标准开场
> - **I was wondering if...** — 委婉询问，比直接问 Are you open 更礼貌
> - **Did you want to...?** — 过去时表委婉建议，不是真的问过去
> - **I'll put you down for...** — 帮你登记，和 under [name] 对应
> - **We'll see you then** — 电话挂断标准用语，替代死板的 Goodbye

## 对话 7：改时间

**A:** Hi, I have a reservation for Friday at seven, under Amy Liu. But we need to push it back to eight. Is that possible?  
**B:** Let me see... Friday at eight... we can do that. I've updated it.  
**A:** Great, everything else stays the same. Thanks.  
**B:** No problem. See you at eight, Ms. Liu.

> **语言点**
> - **push it back to [time]** — 把时间往后推（延后）。如果是提前用 move it up to [time]
> - **Is that possible?** — 委婉请求，比 Can you 更有礼貌
> - **everything else stays the same** — 其他不变，简洁表达
