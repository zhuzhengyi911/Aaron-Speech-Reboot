# 结账付款

## 对话 1：要账单

**A (顾客):** Can we get the check, please?  
**B (服务员):** Sure, I'll bring it right over.

> **语言点**
> - **Can we get the check, please?** — 美式要账单。英式用 the bill
> - **I'll bring it right over** — 马上拿过来

## 对话 2：AA 分账

**A:** Can we split the bill?  
**B:** No problem. How would you like to split it?  
**A:** Three ways, evenly.  
**B:** Sure, I'll run three separate cards.

> **语言点**
> - **split the bill** — AA 分账
> - **How would you like to split it?** — 怎么分
> - **evenly** — 平摊
> - **run three separate cards** — 刷三张卡

## 对话 3：各付各的

**A:** We'd like to pay separately.  
**B:** Sure, who's paying for what?  
**A:** I'll take the steak and drinks. She'll take the salad and dessert.  
**B:** Got it, I'll run them as two checks.

> **语言点**
> - **pay separately** — 各付各的
> - **two checks / separate checks** — 分开账单
> - **I'll take [X]** — 我付这部分

## 对话 4：刷卡 / 现金

**A:** Do you take Apple Pay?  
**B:** Yes, we do. Just tap it on the terminal.  
**A:** Done.

> **语言点**
> - **Do you take [Apple Pay / card / cash]?** — 问支付方式
> - **tap it on the terminal** — 在终端上碰一下（NFC 支付）

## 对话 5：给小费

**A:** Should I leave the tip on the machine or in cash?  
**B:** Either way works. The machine will prompt you when you tap your card.  
**A:** Okay, I'll do it on the machine. Twenty percent?  
**B:** Whatever you're comfortable with. We appreciate it either way.

> **语言点**
> - **leave a tip / leave a gratuity** — 给小费
> - **Either way works** — 都行
> - **the machine will prompt you** — 机器会提示你选小费比例
> - **Whatever you're comfortable with** — 你随意

## 对话 6：账单有错

**A:** I think there's a mistake on the bill. We only had two drinks, but it shows three.  
**B:** Let me take a look... You're right, I'm sorry. I'll correct that.  
**A:** Thanks for catching that.

> **语言点**
> - **I think there's a mistake on the bill** — 委婉指出账单错误
> - **Let me take a look** — 我看看
> - **Thanks for catching that** — 谢谢你（对方）指出/改正

## 对话 7：要求开发票 / 收据

**A:** Can I get a receipt?  
**B:** Of course. Do you need it printed or emailed?  
**A:** Printed, please.

> **语言点**
> - **Can I get a receipt?** — 要收据
> - **printed or emailed** — 纸质还是电子版
