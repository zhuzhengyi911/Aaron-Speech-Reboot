# 用餐中途服务

## 对话 1：要纸巾 / 餐具

**A (顾客):** Excuse me, could we get some extra napkins?  
**B (服务员):** Of course, I'll grab those for you.

> **语言点**
> - **Could we get some extra [X]?** — 要东西万能句型
> - **napkins** — 餐巾纸。纸巾也可以说 tissues
> - **I'll grab those for you** — 我去拿

## 对话 2：要 Wi-Fi 密码

**A:** Hi, do you have Wi-Fi here?  
**B:** Yes, the password's on the receipt at your table.  
**A:** Oh, I see it. Thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **Do you have Wi-Fi here?** — 简单直接
> - **the password is on...** — 密码在哪里

## 对话 3：水杯续水

**A:** Could we get some more water?  
**B:** Sure, I'll top it off.

> **语言点**
> - **top it off** — 加满（杯子/酒杯都适用）
> - **Could we get some more water?** — 续水说法

## 对话 4：催菜

**A:** Excuse me, we ordered about thirty minutes ago. How much longer for our food?  
**B:** Let me check on that for you... It's coming right up.  
**A:** Okay, thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **How much longer for our food?** — 催菜委婉说法
> - **Let me check on that for you** — 我帮你查一下
> - **It's coming right up** — 马上就好

## 对话 5：要调料

**A:** Do you have any hot sauce?  
**B:** Sure, I'll bring some over.

> **语言点**
> - **Do you have any [X]?** — 要调料万能问法
> - hot sauce / soy sauce / ketchup / chili flakes 等

## 对话 6：菜太烫

**A:** This soup is really hot. Can I get a spoon?  
**B:** Of course. Be careful — it stays hot for a while.

> **语言点**
> - **This is really hot** — 太烫了
> - **it stays hot for a while** — 会烫一会儿
