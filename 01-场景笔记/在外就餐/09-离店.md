# 离店

## 对话 1：打包剩菜

**A (顾客):** Can we get a box for the leftovers?  
**B (服务员):** Sure, I'll bring one out.

> **语言点**
> - **Can we get a box for the leftovers?** — 打包剩菜标准说法
> - **leftovers** — 剩菜剩饭
> - **a box / a to-go box / a doggy bag** — 打包盒

## 对话 2：道别离开

**A:** Everything was great, thanks.  
**B:** Glad to hear it! Have a good night.  
**A:** You too, see ya.

> **语言点**
> - **Everything was great** — 用餐体验好的通用表达
> - **Glad to hear it** — 服务员回应好评
> - **Have a good night / Have a good one** — 道别万能句

## 对话 3：下次还会来

**A:** This was really good. We'll definitely be back.  
**B:** That's wonderful to hear. We look forward to seeing you again.  
**A:** Thanks!

> **语言点**
> - **We'll definitely be back** — 我们一定会再来
> - **We look forward to seeing you again** — 服务方标准回应

## 对话 4：落了东西

**A:** Hi, I think I left my umbrella at our table. Have you seen it?  
**B:** Let me check the lost and found... Yes, we have it here.  
**A:** Great, I'll swing by to pick it up tomorrow.

> **语言点**
> - **I left my [X] at our table** — 我落了东西
> - **lost and found** — 失物招领处
> - **swing by** — 顺路来取

## 对话 5：问能不能存酒

**A:** We have an unfinished bottle of wine. Can you hold it for us?  
**B:** Yes, we can store it for up to thirty days. Just let us know when you come back.  
**A:** Perfect, thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **Can you hold it for us?** — 帮我们留着
> - **store it for up to [X] days** — 最多存多少天
