# 问题处理

## 对话 1：菜上错了

**A (顾客):** Excuse me, I ordered the chicken, but this is fish.  
**B (服务员):** Oh, I'm so sorry. Let me double-check your order... You're right, this isn't yours. I'll get your chicken right away.  
**A:** No worries, take your time.

> **语言点**
> - **I ordered X, but this is Y** — 上错菜标准说法
> - **Let me double-check** — 我核实一下
> - **No worries, take your time** — 大度回应，不急

## 对话 2：菜没熟

**A:** Sorry, my steak is pretty rare. I asked for medium.  
**B:** I'm sorry about that. I'll take it back to the kitchen and get it cooked properly.  
**A:** Thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **I asked for [X]** — 我点的是…（暗示对方搞错了）
> - **take it back to the kitchen** — 拿回后厨重做
> - **rare / medium / well-done** — 牛排熟度

## 对话 3：菜里有异物

**A:** Excuse me, there's a hair in my food.  
**B:** Oh my gosh, I'm so sorry. Let me get you a fresh one right away.  
**A:** Thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **There's a [X] in my food** — 菜里有异物的标准说法
> - **Oh my gosh** — 服务方表示惊讶+抱歉
> - **a fresh one** — 重新做一份

## 对话 4：菜太咸 / 口味不对

**A:** This is a bit too salty for me. Is there any way to fix it?  
**B:** I'm sorry to hear that. I can have the kitchen make a new one, or I can take it off your bill.  
**A:** A new one would be great, thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **a bit too [salty / sweet / spicy] for me** — 委婉表达口味不合
> - **Is there any way to fix it?** — 有没有办法解决
> - **take it off your bill** — 这道菜免单

## 对话 5：等太久要求退菜

**A:** We've been waiting for forty minutes for our main course. At this point, we'd like to cancel that dish.  
**B:** I completely understand. I'll have it removed from your bill.  
**A:** Thank you.

> **语言点**
> - **At this point** — 到这个地步了（表示不满）
> - **we'd like to cancel that dish** — 要求退菜
> - **removed from your bill** — 从账单中移除

## 对话 6：要求见经理

**A:** I'd like to speak with the manager, please.  
**B:** Of course, let me get them for you.

> **语言点**
> - **I'd like to speak with the manager** — 要求见经理，礼貌但正式
> - 通常在其他解决方式都无效时使用
