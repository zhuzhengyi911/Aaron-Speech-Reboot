# 看菜单 / 要推荐

## 对话 1：问推荐菜

**A (顾客):** What's good here?  
**B (服务员):** I'd recommend the ribeye — it's our most popular dish.  
**A:** Sounds good. How big is the portion?  
**B:** It's pretty generous, good for sharing.

> **语言点**
> - **What's good here?** — 万能问推荐句，不限于餐厅
> - **I'd recommend...** — 委婉推荐，加了 'd 比直接说 I recommend 更自然
> - **How big is the portion?** — 问分量大小
> - **generous** — 形容分量足，比 big / large 更地道

> **扩展：一次性说完**
>
> **A:** Hi, what's good here? I'd love a recommendation.  
> **B:** I'd go with the ribeye — it's our signature dish.  
> **A:** I'll take it.

## 对话 2：看不懂菜单

**A:** Sorry, is there an English menu? This one's all in French.  
**B:** Of course, let me grab one for you.  
**A:** Thanks. Also, what does "coq au vin" mean?  
**B:** It's chicken braised in red wine — basically a stew.

> **语言点**
> - **Is there an English menu?** — 出国常问
> - **let me grab one** — grab 表示快速去拿
> - **what does [dish] mean?** — 问菜名含义
> - **basically a stew** — basically 用来通俗解释陌生菜

## 对话 3：问菜里有什么 / 过敏

**A:** Does the Caesar salad come with chicken?  
**B:** It comes with romaine lettuce, croutons, and parmesan. Chicken is extra.  
**A:** Got it. And does the sauce have nuts? I'm allergic.  
**B:** No nuts in the Caesar dressing, but I'll let the kitchen know just in case.

> **语言点**
> - **Does it come with...?** — 问菜品包含内容
> - **extra** — 额外加钱：Chicken is extra = 加鸡肉要加钱
> - **I'm allergic** — 过敏声明，后面可以加 to nuts / to shellfish
> - **just in case** — 以防万一

## 对话 4：纠结选哪个

**A:** I can't decide between the salmon and the pasta. Which one would you go with?  
**B:** If you like seafood, the salmon's fresh today. But the pasta is a solid choice too.  
**A:** I'll go with the salmon then.

> **语言点**
> - **I can't decide between A and B** — 纠结两个选哪个
> - **Which one would you go with?** — 问对方推荐，比 which is better 更口语
> - **a solid choice** — 靠谱的选择，不出错
> - **I'll go with...** — 做决定万能句型

## 对话 5：问今日特色

**A:** Do you have any specials today?  
**B:** Yes, our daily special is grilled sea bass with roasted vegetables.  
**A:** What comes with it?  
**B:** Soup or salad, and it includes a drink.

> **语言点**
> - **Do you have any specials today?** — 问当日特色
> - **daily special** — 每日特供
> - **What comes with it?** — 问搭配，点餐高频

## 对话 6：菜单上没有的

**A:** Do you guys have anything that's not on the menu? Like a secret special?  
**B:** (laughs) We do have a chef's special today — truffle mushroom risotto. It's not on the board.  
**A:** I'll try that.

> **语言点**
> - **Do you guys have...** — 口语化问法，guys 不指性别，就是"你们"
> - **not on the menu / not on the board** — 菜单上没有的
> - **chef's special** — 主厨特推
