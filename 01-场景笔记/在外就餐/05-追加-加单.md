# 追加 / 加单

## 对话 1：加菜

**A (顾客):** Excuse me, can we order one more dish?  
**B (服务员):** Of course. What would you like?  
**A:** We'd like the garlic bread.  
**B:** Sure, I'll put that in for you. It'll be about ten minutes.

> **语言点**
> - **Excuse me** — 叫服务员的标准方式，比 Hey 礼貌
> - **can we order one more dish?** — 加菜直接说法
> - **I'll put that in for you** — 我帮你下单
> - **It'll be about [X] minutes** — 告知等待时间

## 对话 2：加点饮料

**A:** Hey, can we get two more beers?  
**B:** Sure thing. Same brand?  
**A:** Yeah, the same.

> **语言点**
> - **can we get...?** — 加饮料万能说法，get 比 order 口语化
> - **Sure thing** — 比 Sure 更热情
> - **Same brand?** — 同一种吗？

## 对话 3：要菜单再加一轮

**A:** Can we see the menu again? We might want to order another round.  
**B:** I'll bring it right over.

> **语言点**
> - **another round** — 再来一轮（点菜/点酒都适用）
> - **I'll bring it right over** — 马上拿过来

## 对话 4：追加米饭 / 主食

**A:** Can we get a refill on the rice?  
**B:** Absolutely. I'll bring some extra.

> **语言点**
> - **a refill on [X]** — 续加。饮料 / 米饭 / 汤都可以用
> - **extra rice / extra fries** — 也可以直接说 extra + 食物

## 对话 5：加单被拒绝（已售罄）

**A:** We'd like to order the salmon.  
**B:** I'm sorry, we actually just ran out of that. Can I suggest something else?  
**A:** Oh, that's a shame. What else do you recommend?  
**B:** The sea bass is excellent — very similar.  
**A:** Okay, we'll try that instead.

> **语言点**
> - **we ran out of that** — 卖完了。也可以说 we're out of [X] / we've sold out
> - **That's a shame** — 可惜
> - **we'll try that instead** — 那换这个
