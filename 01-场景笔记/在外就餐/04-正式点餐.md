# 正式点餐

## 对话 1：正常点餐

**A (服务员):** Are you ready to order?  
**B (顾客):** Yeah, I'll have the chicken burger.  
**A:** How would you like your burger done?  
**B:** Medium, please.  
**A:** Would you like fries with that?  
**B:** Yeah, I'll take fries.

> **语言点**
> - **Are you ready to order?** — 服务员标准问法
> - **I'll have...** — 点餐万能句型，比 I want 礼貌一百倍
> - **How would you like it done?** — 问牛排/汉堡熟度
> - **I'll take...** — 和 I'll have 同义，交替使用避免重复

## 对话 2：多点几样

**A:** So for the main, I'd like the steak. And can I also get the soup as a side?  
**B:** Sure, that comes with a side of your choice. Would you like soup or salad?  
**A:** Soup, please.  
**B:** Great. Anything to drink?  
**A:** Just water for now.

> **语言点**
> - **can I also get... as a side?** — 加点配菜
> - **that comes with a side of your choice** — 套餐含一份自选配菜
> - **Just [X] for now** — 先只要这个，后面可能还会加

## 对话 3：点餐时提要求

**A:** I'd like the grilled salmon, but can I get the sauce on the side?  
**B:** No problem, sauce on the side.  
**A:** And can you make it less salty?  
**B:** I'll pass that along to the kitchen.

> **语言点**
> - **can I get the sauce on the side?** — 酱汁分开放
> - **on the side** — 配菜/酱料单独放，不拌在一起
> - **less salty / extra crispy / no onions** — 点餐时提口味要求
> - **I'll pass that along to the kitchen** — 我转告后厨

## 对话 4：多人一起点

**A (服务员):** Who'd like to start?  
**B:** I'll go with the Caesar salad.  
**C:** Same for me.  
**D:** I'll have the tomato soup.  
**A (服务员):** Great. And for the mains?

> **语言点**
> - **Who'd like to start?** — 服务员让客人轮流点
> - **Same for me / I'll have the same** — 我也要一样的
> - **for the mains** — main course 主菜

## 对话 5：点餐没听清 / 没看懂

**A:** And today's soup is French onion.  
**B:** Sorry, what was that?  
**A:** French onion soup. It's a classic.  
**B:** Oh, I'll take that. Thanks.

> **语言点**
> - **Sorry, what was that?** — 没听清时万能追问，比 Pardon 自然
> - **Sorry, can you say that again?** — 另一种说法

## 对话 6：点餐被推荐

**A:** I'll have the chicken pasta.  
**B:** Good choice. We actually just got fresh truffles in — we can shave some on top for an extra three dollars.  
**A:** Oh, that sounds amazing. Yes, please.

> **语言点**
> - **Good choice** — 服务员肯定客人选择
> - **we just got [X] in** — 刚进货/刚到货
> - **shave some on top** — 削一点在上面（truffle 常用搭配）
