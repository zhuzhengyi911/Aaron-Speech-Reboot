## 完整对话文本

**Waiter:** Good evening, my name is Fabio. I'll be your waiter for tonight. May I take your order?

**Customer:** No, I'm still working on it. This menu is not even in English. What's good here?

**Waiter:** For you, sir, I would recommend spaghetti and meatballs.

**Customer:** Does it come with coke and fries?

**Waiter:** It comes with either soup or salad, and a complimentary glass of wine, sir.

**Customer:** I'll go with the spaghetti and meatballs, salad and the wine.

**Waiter:** Excellent choice. Your order will be ready soon.

**Customer:** How soon is soon?

**Waiter:** 20 minutes.

**Customer:** You know what? I'll just go grab a burger across the street.

## Key Vocabulary

### still working on
**含义**：还在处理中，还没完成。不是真的在"工作"，而是一种"我还没搞定"的地道说法，常用于对方催你时拖延时间。

- 原文：*No, I'm still working on it.*（还没想好点什么）
- 例句：Are you done with the report? — Not yet, I'm still working on it.
- 例句：Have you decided where to go? — Still working on it.

### would recommend
**含义**：推荐。比直接说 "I recommend" 更委婉礼貌，加了 would 显得更有建议性而不是命令感。

- 原文：*For you, sir, I would recommend spaghetti and meatballs.*
- 例句：What should I watch? — I would recommend that new sci-fi movie.
- 例句：If you're a beginner, I would recommend starting with the basics.

### complimentary
**含义**：免费的、赠送的（通常指商家附送的）。比 free 高级，听起来更有档次，餐厅酒店常用。

- 原文：*...and a complimentary glass of wine, sir.*
- 例句：The hotel offers complimentary breakfast.
- 例句：You get a complimentary drink with your meal.

### go with
**含义**：选择、决定用某个选项。不是字面的"跟…一起走"，而是 "我选这个" 的口语说法。

- 原文：*I'll go with the spaghetti and meatballs, salad and the wine.*
- 例句：Which color do you want? — I'll go with black.
- 例句：We have two plans. — Let's go with the cheaper one.

### grab
**含义**：快速地去拿、快速地做。不是"抓取"，而是强调动作快、随手、不用太正式。

- 原文：*I'll just go grab a burger across the street.*
- 例句：Let me grab my laptop real quick.
- 例句：I'm gonna grab a coffee, wanna come?
